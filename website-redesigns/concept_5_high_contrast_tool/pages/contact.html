QUICK DIAGNOSTICS

> Before submitting a ticket, try these common solutions:

ISSUE QUICK FIX
Cannot connect to adapter Re-pair Bluetooth. Check adapter LED. Verify OBD port has power (key ON).
NO DATA on all PIDs Open Terminal. Send ATZ then ATS1 then ATCAF1. Retry scan.
Connection drops frequently Disable battery optimization for ODB+ in Android Settings.
BUS INIT: ERROR Send ATZ. Do not send ATSH commands on ISO/KWP protocols.
App crashes on startup Clear app data: Settings > Apps > ODB+ > Clear Data. Reinstall if needed.

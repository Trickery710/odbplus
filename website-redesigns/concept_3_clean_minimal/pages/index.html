How It Works

Three steps to clarity.

Plug In

Insert any ELM327-compatible OBD-II adapter into your vehicle's diagnostic port. It is located under the dashboard on the driver's side in every car made since 1996.

Connect

Open ODB+ and tap connect. The app automatically discovers your adapter via Bluetooth or WiFi, identifies your vehicle's protocol, and establishes a live data link in seconds.

Understand

View live sensor data, read and clear trouble codes, run guided tests, or ask the AI assistant to explain what your car is telling you. No mechanic jargon required.

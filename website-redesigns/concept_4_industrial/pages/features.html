Features

Every tool a diagnostic professional needs

AI-Powered Diagnostics

Chat with an artificial intelligence that has real-time access to your vehicle's ECU data. Describe your symptoms -- "rough idle at cold start", "hesitation under load" -- and the AI correlates your description with live sensor readings to identify probable causes. It can suggest specific tests, explain fault codes in context, and recommend repair procedures ranked by likelihood.

Natural language Real-time data access Repair suggestions Severity grading
Active

Live Data Monitoring

Stream real-time sensor data from your vehicle's ECU at up to 200ms refresh rates. Monitor RPM, coolant temperature, O2 sensor voltage, MAF, MAP, fuel trims, timing advance, throttle position, and dozens more PIDs. View data as analog gauges, digital readouts, time-series graphs, or raw hex. Configure custom dashboards with the parameters that matter to your diagnosis.

200ms refresh 40+ PIDs Gauge / Graph / Raw Custom dashboards
Active

18,000+ Error Code Database

Comprehensive DTC library covering generic OBD-II codes (P0xxx, B0xxx, C0xxx, U0xxx) and manufacturer-specific codes for every major automaker. Each code includes a plain-language description, probable causes ranked by likelihood, step-by-step repair procedures, severity rating, and related technical service bulletins. Read stored, pending, and permanent codes. Clear codes after repair.

18,247 codes 50+ manufacturers Repair guides TSB references
Active

Raw Terminal Mode

Direct command-line access to your ELM327 adapter. Send AT configuration commands, raw OBD-II PIDs, enhanced diagnostic commands, and hex-level protocol data. View unfiltered responses with header bytes, timestamps, and protocol metadata. Essential for developers, tuners, and advanced diagnosticians who need to see exactly what the ECU is saying.

AT commands Raw hex I/O Header display Command history
Advanced

Session Logger & Replay

Record complete diagnostic sessions with timestamped sensor data, DTCs, and command logs. Replay any past session to review data trends over time. Compare before/after snapshots to verify repairs. Export sessions as CSV for spreadsheet analysis or share them directly in the ODB+ community forums for peer review.

Timestamped recording Replay engine CSV export Session sharing

Guided Tests & Diagnostic HUD

Automated, step-by-step diagnostic procedures that walk you through common tests. RPM stability test, O2 sensor response test, catalyst efficiency check, and more. The Diagnostic HUD overlays key readings on your screen while you drive, giving you real-time feedback during test drives without switching between screens.

RPM test O2 response Catalyst check HUD overlay

Vehicle-Specific Forums

Community forums organized by manufacturer and model. Share diagnostic sessions, ask questions, and get answers from experienced mechanics and enthusiasts who know your specific vehicle. Browse 50+ manufacturer boards covering everything from common problems to performance modifications.

Sensor Reference & Parts Catalog

Comprehensive reference library for OBD-II sensors with specifications, testing procedures, wiring diagrams, and expected voltage/resistance ranges. Integrated parts catalog for quick lookup of replacement sensors, connectors, and related components. From diagnosis to repair in one workflow.

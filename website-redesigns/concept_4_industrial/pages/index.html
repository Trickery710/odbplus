Procedure

How It Works

01

Plug In

Connect an ELM327-compatible OBD-II adapter to your vehicle's diagnostic port (under the dashboard, driver's side).

02

Pair

Open ODB+ and tap Connect. The app auto-detects your adapter via Bluetooth or WiFi and establishes a secure data link.

03

Diagnose

Read live sensor data, pull error codes, run guided tests, or ask the AI assistant about your vehicle's health.

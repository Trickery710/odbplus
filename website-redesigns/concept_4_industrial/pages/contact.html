Common Questions

Support FAQ

What information should I include in a bug report?
Include your Android version, ODB+ app version (visible in Settings), adapter model and firmware version (send ATI in Terminal Mode), vehicle year/make/model, detected protocol (ATDP), and a description of the steps to reproduce the issue. Attach your session log export if possible -- this dramatically speeds up diagnosis.
How do I request a new feature?
Use the contact form above with "Feature Request" as the subject, or post in the Feature Requests board on the community forums. Describe the use case, not just the feature -- telling us why you need it helps us prioritize and design the right solution. Feature requests are reviewed weekly and the most impactful ones are added to the development roadmap.
Is my adapter compatible with ODB+?
ODB+ supports all ELM327 v1.3+ compatible adapters (including clones), STN1110/STN2120 chipsets, OBDLink series (MX+, LX, EX, SX), and ESP32-based WiFi adapters. If your adapter responds to the ATI command with an ELM327 or STN identifier, it is compatible. Check the adapter setup documentation for the full compatibility list.
Do you offer enterprise or fleet licensing?
Yes. For fleet operations requiring multiple device deployments, volume licensing, or custom feature development, contact us using the "Business Inquiry" subject in the form above. Include the number of devices, fleet size, and any specific requirements. We offer per-seat pricing with priority support for commercial operations.

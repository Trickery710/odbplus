Features

Everything You Need to Diagnose

Professional-grade vehicle diagnostics with an intuitive interface. From live data to AI analysis, ODB+ covers every aspect of modern OBD-II diagnostics.

AI Diagnostics

Chat with an AI assistant that understands your vehicle's live data. Get instant repair recommendations and root cause analysis.

Live Monitoring

Real-time streaming of RPM, temperatures, O2 sensors, MAF, MAP, fuel trims, and dozens more with configurable layouts.

Error Code Database

Access 18,000+ DTCs with full descriptions, probable causes, repair guides, and difficulty ratings.

Session Logger

Record diagnostic sessions with full PID data for later replay. Export logs as CSV or share with your mechanic.

Terminal Mode

Direct access to the OBD-II command interface. Send raw AT and PID commands and view hex responses.

Guided Tests

Step-by-step diagnostic procedures like RPM tests, HUD overlays, and health check routines with pass/fail scoring.

How It Works

Three Steps to Diagnosis

No complicated setup. No subscription walls. Just plug in your adapter, pair with Bluetooth, and start diagnosing.

1

Connect

Plug any ELM327-compatible OBD-II adapter into your vehicle's diagnostic port and pair it via Bluetooth or WiFi.

2

Scan

ODB+ automatically detects your vehicle's protocol, discovers supported sensors, and begins reading live data within seconds.

3

Diagnose

View real-time data, read trouble codes, chat with AI for repair advice, or run guided tests to pinpoint issues.

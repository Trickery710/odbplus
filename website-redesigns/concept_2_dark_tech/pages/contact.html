FAQ

Common Questions

ODB+ works with any ELM327-compatible Bluetooth or WiFi adapter. This includes genuine ELM327 v2.x chips, STN1100/STN2100 adapters, and ESP32-based custom adapters. Cheap v1.5 clones may have limited functionality. We recommend adapters with genuine v2.1+ firmware.
ODB+ supports all OBD-II compliant vehicles. In the US, this includes all gasoline cars from 1996 and diesel from 1997. In Europe, gasoline from 2001 and diesel from 2004. All five OBD-II protocols are supported: CAN (ISO 15765-4), KWP2000 (ISO 14230-4), ISO 9141-2, J1850 PWM, and J1850 VPW.

How It Works

Three Steps to Diagnose

01

Pair Your Adapter

> Bluetooth.scan("ELM327")

Plug any ELM327-compatible OBD-II adapter into your vehicle's diagnostic port. Open ODB+ and pair via Bluetooth or WiFi. Auto-detection handles the rest.

02

Initialize Protocol

> AdapterSession.negotiate()

ODB+ automatically fingerprints your adapter, detects the vehicle's OBD protocol (CAN, KWP2000, ISO 9141), and establishes a stable communication session.

03

Diagnose

> ObdService.streamPids()

Read live sensor data, pull error codes, run guided tests, or ask the AI for repair advice. Everything updates in real-time with sub-second latency.

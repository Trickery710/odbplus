Common Issues by Generation

11th Gen (2023-Present) - CV

  • Infotainment bugs - Google Built-in glitches, slow response
  • Wind noise - Excessive wind noise at highway speeds
  • Hybrid battery behavior - Learning curve for EV mode
  • Road noise - Tire noise on rough pavement

10th Gen (2018-2022) - CV

  • Oil dilution - 1.5T fuel mixing with oil in cold climates
  • Infotainment lag - Slow touchscreen, random reboots
  • A/C condenser failure - Road debris damage
  • Battery drain - Parasitic draw issues

9th Gen (2013-2017) - CR

  • Vibration at idle - Engine mount failure
  • Battery drain - HandsFreeLink module drain
  • Paint peeling - Clear coat issues on some colors
  • Starter failure - Premature starter motor wear

8th Gen (2008-2012) - CP/CU

  • Excessive oil consumption - 2.4L K24 burns oil
  • Power steering pump whine - Common noise complaint
  • Brake caliper sticking - Uneven brake wear
  • A/C compressor failure - Clutch bearing noise

6th Gen (1998-2002) - CG

  • Transmission failure - Auto trans slipping (V6 models)
  • Ignition switch recall - Electrical issues
  • EGR valve clogging - Rough idle, check engine light
  • Rear subframe rust - Corrosion in salt states

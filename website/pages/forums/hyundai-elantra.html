Common Issues by Generation

2021-Present

  • 1.6T oil consumption - Theta2 or Gamma2 turbo piston ring wear
  • IVT transmission - Wet-clutch IVT (DCT) shudder in slow traffic
  • Wireless charging pad - Qi charging drops and phone overheating
  • LED headlamp fog - Moisture intrusion into LED projection units

2011-2016

  • Theta II engine recall - Bearing failure causing engine seizure on affected VINs
  • Power steering noise - Electric motor hum at low speed
  • Sunroof wind noise - Wind buffeting at highway speed
  • Oil cap gasket - Oil cap sealing face gasket early wear

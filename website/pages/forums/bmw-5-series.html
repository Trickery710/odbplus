Common Issues by Generation

G30/G31/F90 (2017-Present)

  • iDrive issues - Software bugs, connectivity problems
  • Oil consumption - B58 may burn oil
  • 48V mild hybrid - Battery/starter generator faults
  • Air suspension - Compressor failure (models with air ride)

F10/F11/F07 (2010-2017)

  • Timing chain stretch - N20/N26 chain guides fail
  • High pressure fuel pump - N55 HPFP failure
  • Water pump failure - Electric water pump common
  • Oil filter housing leak - Gasket deterioration
  • Turbo wastegate - N55 wastegate rattle

E60/E61 (2003-2010)

  • VANOS failure - Variable valve timing issues (N52/N54)
  • Water pump failure - Electric water pump
  • Valve cover gasket - Oil leak
  • iDrive issues - CCC/CIC controller failure
  • SMG pump failure - M5 sequential manual gearbox

E39 (1996-2003)

  • Cooling system - Expansion tank, thermostat, water pump
  • Window regulator - Cable breaks
  • Control arm bushings - Front suspension wear
  • Rear subframe mounts - Rubber bushing deterioration
  • Pixel fade - Instrument cluster display

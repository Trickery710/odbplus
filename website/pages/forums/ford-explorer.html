Common Issues by Generation

6th Gen (2020-Present) - U625

  • Rear camera recall - Backup camera cable chafing
  • 10-speed transmission - Harsh shifts, clunking
  • SYNC 3/4 bugs - Infotainment freezing, slow response
  • Roof rail seals - Water leak into cabin
  • ST exhaust drone - Highway drone complaints

5th Gen (2011-2019) - U502

  • PTU failure - Power transfer unit leaks, whine
  • Water pump leak - 3.5L internal coolant leak
  • Transmission shudder - 6F50/55 torque converter issues
  • Door latch recall - Door may open while driving
  • Carbon buildup - EcoBoost intake valve deposits

4th Gen (2006-2010) - U251

  • Timing chain rattle - 4.0L SOHC timing chain guides
  • Transmission shudder - 5R55S torque converter
  • Blend door actuator - HVAC clicking, no heat/AC
  • Throttle body - Electronic throttle issues
  • Power window - Regulator motor failure

3rd Gen (2002-2005) - U152

  • Timing chain rattle - 4.0L SOHC chain guides fail
  • Transmission failure - 5R55S/W valve body issues
  • Rear differential - IRS diff failure on AWD models
  • Blend door actuator - HVAC clicking noise
  • Ball joint recall - Front suspension wear

2nd Gen (1995-2001) - UN105/UN150

  • Transmission failure - 5R55E valve body, overdrive servo
  • Timing chain rattle - 4.0L OHV tensioner failure
  • Intake manifold gasket - Coolant leak (5.0L V8)
  • Ball joint wear - Front suspension issues
  • Fuel pump failure - In-tank pump failure

Common Issues by Generation

2011-2020 (5th Gen)

  • 3.6L timing chain - Cam phaser rattle, especially cold start
  • Power sliding door - Door motor and track failure
  • 6-speed trans - Torque converter shudder and harsh shifts
  • Window regulator - Clip breakage causing glass to drop

2001-2007

  • Transmission failure - 4-speed auto failure common after 100k
  • Head gasket - 3.3L coolant leak into combustion
  • TIPM failure - Total Integrated Power Module causing no-start
  • Lower control arm - Bushing wear causing steering wander

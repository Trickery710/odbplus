Common Issues by Generation

5th Gen (2019-Present) - XA50

  • Fuel pump recall - Denso low-pressure fuel pump failure
  • Transmission hesitation - Delayed engagement from stop
  • Liftgate malfunction - Power liftgate won't open/close
  • Phantom braking - TSS activates unexpectedly (Prime)
  • Battery drain - 12V battery drain on Prime models

4th Gen (2013-2018) - XA40

  • Transmission shudder - Vibration during acceleration
  • Water leak - Sunroof drain clogs, water in cabin
  • Bluetooth issues - Entune connectivity problems
  • A/C odor - Musty smell from evaporator

3rd Gen (2006-2012) - XA30

  • Excessive oil consumption - 2.4L 2AZ-FE burns oil
  • VVT-i rattle - Cold start noise from VVT system
  • Dashboard cracking - Dash cracks in hot climates
  • ECM failure - Engine computer issues on V6 models

2nd Gen (2001-2005) - XA20

  • ECM capacitor failure - Random stalling, no-start
  • Sludge buildup - Oil sludge on early 2.4L engines
  • Transfer case actuator - 4WD engagement issues
  • Oxygen sensor failure - Common on high-mileage vehicles

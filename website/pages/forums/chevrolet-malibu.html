Common Issues by Generation

9th Gen (2016-2024) - E2XX

  • CVT shudder - Transmission vibration at low speeds
  • Excessive oil consumption - 1.5L Turbo burns oil
  • Infotainment issues - MyLink screen flickering, freezing
  • Start-stop problems - Auto start-stop malfunction
  • Battery drain - Parasitic draw issues

8th Gen (2013-2015) - Epsilon II

  • Transmission shudder - 6-speed harsh shifts
  • Power steering failure - Electric power steering issues
  • Infotainment bugs - MyLink system crashes
  • Key fob issues - Keyless entry problems
  • A/C odor - Musty smell from evaporator

7th Gen (2008-2012) - Epsilon

  • Excessive oil consumption - 2.4L Ecotec burns oil
  • Timing chain stretch - 2.4L timing chain wear
  • Stabilitrak issues - Service message, reduced power
  • Power steering - Electric power steering failure
  • Camshaft actuator - VVT solenoid issues

6th Gen (2004-2008) - Epsilon

  • Passlock issues - Security system prevents starting
  • Power steering - Electric power steering failure
  • Timing chain rattle - 3.5L/3.9L V6 chain tensioner
  • Intake manifold gasket - 3.5L coolant leak
  • Wheel speed sensor - ABS/traction control issues

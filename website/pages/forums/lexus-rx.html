Common Issues by Generation

5th Gen (2023-Present) - AL30

  • Infotainment bugs - Lexus Interface software glitches
  • Wind noise - Door seal issues at highway speeds
  • PHEV charging - Charging system faults
  • Turbo lag - 2.4L turbo response time

4th Gen (2016-2022) - AL20

  • Dashboard cracking - Sun damage to dashboard
  • Fuel injector tick - Direct injection noise
  • Transmission shudder - 8-speed auto hesitation
  • Infotainment lag - Remote Touch interface slow
  • Water pump leak - Coolant leaks

3rd Gen (2010-2015) - AL10

  • Oil consumption - 2GR-FE V6 burning oil

  • AC evaporator - Musty smell, mold

  • Dashboard cracking - Heat damage

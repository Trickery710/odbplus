Common Issues by Generation

14th Gen (2021-Present) - P702

  • Windshield wiper motor recall - Wiper motor may fail
  • Soft-close tailgate - Latching issues on some models
  • SYNC 4 bugs - Infotainment glitches, slow response
  • Lightning charging issues - Charging station compatibility
  • 10-speed transmission - Harsh shifts, clunking

13th Gen (2015-2020) - P552

  • 10-speed transmission - Harsh shifts, hunting for gears
  • Cam phaser tick - 5.0L V8 timing chain rattle
  • EcoBoost coolant leak - 3.5L EcoBoost coolant intrusion
  • EPAS shudder - Electric power steering vibration
  • Exhaust manifold leak - 5.0L manifold studs break

12th Gen (2009-2014) - P415

  • Cam phaser tick - 5.0L V8 cold start rattle
  • EPAS failure - Electric power steering motor failure
  • Transmission shudder - 6-speed harsh shifts
  • Spark plug ejection - 5.4L threads strip
  • Timing chain stretch - 3.5L EcoBoost timing issues

11th Gen (2004-2008) - P221

  • Spark plug ejection - 5.4L 3V plugs break during removal
  • Cam phaser failure - 5.4L VCT timing issues
  • Window regulator failure - Power windows fail
  • Fuel pump driver module - Corrosion causes no-start
  • Exhaust manifold studs - Broken studs cause exhaust leak

10th Gen (1997-2003) - P131

  • Spark plug blowout - 4.6L/5.4L 2V plug threads strip
  • Intake manifold crack - Plastic manifold cracks (4.6L)
  • Idle air control - Rough idle, stalling
  • Ball joint wear - Front suspension wear
  • Transmission slip - 4R70W/4R100 issues

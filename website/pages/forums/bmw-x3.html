Common Issues by Generation

G01/F97 (2018-Present)

  • iDrive issues - Software bugs, connectivity problems
  • Oil consumption - B48/B58 may burn oil
  • 48V mild hybrid - Battery/starter generator issues
  • xDrive issues - Transfer case noise/vibration

F25 (2011-2017)

  • Timing chain stretch - N20 4-cylinder chain guides fail
  • Water pump failure - Electric water pump
  • Oil filter housing leak - Gasket deterioration
  • High pressure fuel pump - N55 HPFP issues
  • xDrive transfer case - Actuator motor failure

E83 (2003-2010)

  • Cooling system - Expansion tank, thermostat, water pump
  • Transfer case failure - Actuator motor, servo
  • Power steering leak - Reservoir and hoses
  • VANOS failure - Oil seals deteriorate
  • Valve cover gasket - Oil leak common

Common Issues by Generation

4th Gen (2020-Present) - CX482

  • PHEV recall - Fire risk when parked (2020-2021 PHEV)
  • Infotainment glitches - SYNC 3 freezing, slow response
  • CVT behavior - Hybrid CVT learning curve
  • Oil consumption - 1.5L EcoBoost oil use reports
  • Road noise - Tire noise complaints

3rd Gen (2013-2019) - C520

  • Coolant leak - 1.6L EcoBoost internal coolant leak
  • Transmission shudder - 6F35 torque converter issues
  • Carbon buildup - EcoBoost intake valve deposits
  • Door latch recall - Door may open while driving
  • PTU failure - AWD power transfer unit whine

2nd Gen (2008-2012) - C307

  • Throttle body - Electronic throttle issues
  • Transmission shudder - CD4E torque converter failure
  • Power steering - Electric power steering failure
  • Blend door actuator - HVAC clicking noise
  • Ignition coils - Coil pack failure (V6)

1st Gen (2001-2007) - CD2

  • Transmission failure - CD4E valve body, overdrive issues
  • Throttle position sensor - Hesitation, rough idle
  • Ball joint wear - Front suspension issues
  • Coil pack failure - Ignition coil issues
  • Alternator failure - Premature wear

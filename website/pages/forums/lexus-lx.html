Common Issues by Generation

2022-Present (LX 600)

  • 3.4T GR oil consumption - New engine break-in oil consumption
  • 10-speed calibration - Gear hunting on steep grades when loaded
  • Air suspension - Front-to-rear leveling speed
  • Multimedia system - 12.3" screen connectivity drops with Apple CarPlay

2008-2021 (LX 570)

  • KDSS leak - Kinetic Dynamic Suspension System oil leak
  • 5.7L VVT-i noise - Cold-start VVT noise normal for this engine
  • Running board motor - Power running board actuator failure
  • Rear differential - Axle seal and pinion seal replacement at 150k+

1998-2007 (LX 470)

  • 4.7L timing belt - Critical service at 60k/90k intervals
  • Center diff lock - Electromagnetic center differential actuator failure
  • AC compressor - Sanden compressor failure on high-mileage units
  • Roof rack rust - Factory rack attachment point corrosion

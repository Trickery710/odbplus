Common Issues by Generation

G20/G21/G80/G81 (2019-Present)

  • iDrive issues - Software bugs, slow response
  • Oil consumption - B48/B58 may burn oil
  • 48V mild hybrid - Battery/starter generator issues
  • Adaptive suspension - Electronic damper faults

F30/F31/F34/F80 (2012-2019)

  • Timing chain stretch - N20/N26 4-cylinder chain guides
  • High pressure fuel pump - N55 HPFP failure
  • Water pump failure - Electric water pump leaks
  • Oil filter housing leak - Gasket deterioration
  • Turbo wastegate rattle - N55 wastegate actuator

E90/E91/E92/E93 (2006-2013)

  • High pressure fuel pump - N54 HPFP failure
  • Water pump failure - Electric water pump common failure
  • Oil filter housing gasket - Coolant/oil leak
  • VANOS solenoid - Variable valve timing issues
  • Rod bearing failure - E90 M3 S65 V8

E46 (1999-2006)

  • Cooling system failure - Expansion tank, radiator, water pump
  • Rear subframe crack - Mount tears from body
  • Window regulator failure - Cable breaks
  • VANOS failure - Oil seals deteriorate
  • Rod bearing failure - E46 M3 S54

E36 (1992-1999)

  • Cooling system - Radiator, expansion tank, water pump
  • Rear shock mounts - Tower rust-through
  • Control arm bushings - Front suspension wear
  • Window regulator - Cable snaps
  • Idle control valve - Rough idle, stalling

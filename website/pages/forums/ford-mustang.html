Common Issues by Generation

7th Gen (2024-Present) - S650

  • Infotainment glitches - SYNC 4 freezing, slow response
  • Manual trans notchy - MT82-D4 shift feel complaints
  • Dark Horse overheating - Track use thermal management
  • Wind noise - Door seal issues reported

6th Gen (2015-2023) - S550

  • MT82 transmission - Grinding gears, weak synchros
  • 10-speed auto shudder - Harsh shifts, hunting for gears
  • Coyote oil consumption - 5.0L may burn oil at high RPM
  • IRS clunk - Rear suspension noise
  • Clutch chatter - MT82 clutch judder from stop

5th Gen (2005-2014) - S197

  • Spark plug ejection - 4.6L 3V plug thread issues
  • Transmission synchro - TR-3650 2nd gear grind
  • Rear axle whine - 8.8" differential noise
  • Intake manifold runner - IMRC failure (4.6L)
  • Clutch shudder - Factory clutch judder

4th Gen (1994-2004) - SN95

  • Intake manifold crack - Plastic manifold failure
  • Harmonic balancer - Outer ring separates
  • Rear end noise - 7.5"/8.8" axle issues
  • Transmission synchro - T-45 2nd gear grind
  • Head gasket failure - 3.8L V6 prone to failure

3rd Gen (1979-1993) - Fox Body

  • AOD transmission - TV cable adjustment, slipping
  • TFI module failure - Ignition module overheating
  • Fuel pump relay - No-start condition
  • E6 EGR issues - Rough idle, check engine light
  • Radiator leaks - Age-related cooling issues

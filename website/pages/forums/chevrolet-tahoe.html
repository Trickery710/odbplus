Common Issues by Generation

5th Gen (2021-Present) - T1XX

  • Transmission shudder - 10-speed harsh shifts, clunking
  • AFM/DFM lifter failure - 5.3L/6.2L lifter collapse
  • Air suspension issues - Compressor failures (models with air ride)
  • Infotainment bugs - Screen flickering, CarPlay issues
  • Brake pedal travel - Soft brake pedal complaints

4th Gen (2015-2020) - K2XX

  • AFM lifter failure - 5.3L/6.2L V8 lifter collapse
  • Transmission shudder - 8-speed torque converter issues
  • A/C condenser leak - Condenser corrosion
  • Excessive oil consumption - AFM engines burn oil
  • Rear liftgate struts - Struts fail to hold liftgate

3rd Gen (2007-2014) - GMT900

  • AFM lifter failure - 5.3L cylinder deactivation issues
  • Excessive oil consumption - Oil ring issues on AFM engines
  • Stabilitrak/traction control - Service message, reduced power
  • Steering column lock - Ignition lock stuck
  • A/C compressor failure - Clutch bearing wear

2nd Gen (2000-2006) - GMT800

  • Instrument cluster failure - Stepper motor failure
  • Fuel pressure regulator - Hard start, rough idle
  • Intake manifold gasket - Coolant leak (Vortec V8)
  • Secondary air injection - Check engine light, air pump failure
  • 4WD actuator failure - Transfer case encoder motor

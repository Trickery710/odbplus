Common Issues by Generation

11th Gen (2022-Present) - FL/FE

  • Infotainment glitches - Screen freezes, CarPlay disconnects
  • A/C smell - Musty odor from evaporator on new vehicles
  • Side mirror vibration - Shaking at highway speeds
  • Paint quality - Thin paint prone to chips

10th Gen (2016-2021) - FC/FK

  • Oil dilution - 1.5T fuel mixing with oil in cold climates
  • A/C condenser failure - Leaks from road debris damage
  • Infotainment lag - Slow touchscreen response
  • CVT shudder - Vibration during low-speed acceleration

9th Gen (2012-2015) - FB/FG

  • Vibration at idle - Engine mount wear
  • A/C compressor failure - Clutch bearing noise
  • Clear coat peeling - Paint defects on some colors
  • Steering rack leak - Power steering fluid seepage

8th Gen (2006-2011) - FA/FD

  • Cracked engine block - Coolant leak on 1.8L R18 (2006-2009)
  • Power steering pump whine - Noise at low RPM
  • Clear coat failure - Peeling on roof and hood
  • Door lock actuator - Intermittent locking issues

6th Gen (1996-2000) - EJ/EK

  • Distributor failure - Ignition issues, no-start
  • Transmission grind - Synchro wear on manual trans
  • Rear trailing arm rust - Corrosion in northern states
  • A/C compressor - Clutch failure common

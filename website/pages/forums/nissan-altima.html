Common Issues by Generation

6th Gen (2019-Present) - L34

  • CVT shudder - Transmission vibration at low speeds
  • VC-Turbo issues - Variable compression engine concerns
  • Infotainment bugs - NissanConnect glitches
  • Hood flutter - Hood vibration at highway speeds

5th Gen (2013-2018) - L33

  • CVT failure - Transmission shudder, whine, complete failure
  • Excessive oil consumption - 2.5L burns oil
  • Steering lock recall - Steering column lock failure
  • Hood latch corrosion - Secondary latch recall

4th Gen (2007-2012) - L32

  • CVT issues - Transmission shudder, judder, failure
  • Excessive oil consumption - 2.5L QR25DE burns oil
  • Camshaft/crankshaft sensor - No-start condition
  • EVAP system - P0455/P0456 leak codes
  • Steering lock recall - ESCL module failure

3rd Gen (2002-2006) - L31

  • Crankshaft sensor failure - No-start, stalling
  • Catalytic converter failure - P0420 code, theft target
  • Pre-cat failure - 2.5L pre-cat breaks apart
  • Exhaust manifold crack - Tick noise, exhaust smell
  • Timing chain rattle - 2.5L QR25DE chain stretch

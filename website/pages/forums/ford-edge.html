Common Issues by Generation

2nd Gen (2015-2024) - CD539

  • PTU failure - Power transfer unit leaks, whine (AWD)
  • Transmission shudder - 6F50 torque converter issues
  • Coolant leak - 2.0L EcoBoost water pump intrusion
  • SYNC 3 bugs - Infotainment freezing, slow response
  • Door ajar sensor - False door open warning
  • Carbon buildup - EcoBoost intake valve deposits

1st Gen (2007-2014) - CD3

  • Water pump failure - 3.5L/3.7L internal coolant leak
  • Transmission shudder - 6F50/55 torque converter issues
  • PTU failure - AWD system whine, fluid leaks
  • Door latch recall - Door may open while driving
  • Throttle body - Electronic throttle issues
  • A/C compressor - Premature compressor failure

Common Issues by Generation

3rd Gen (2022-Present) - XK70

  • Turbo lag - Delayed throttle response from twin-turbo setup
  • Rear seat folding - Seats don't fold flat properly
  • i-Force Max battery - Hybrid battery concerns in extreme temps
  • Build quality - Interior rattles, panel gaps reported
  • 10-speed transmission - Harsh shifts, hunting for gears

2nd Gen (2007-2021) - XK50

  • Secondary air injection - P0418/P2440/P2442 codes (5.7L V8)
  • Cam tower leak - Oil leak at cam tower gasket (5.7L)
  • Lower ball joint - Premature wear on 4x4 models
  • Exhaust manifold crack - Tick noise from cracked manifold
  • Brake shudder - Warped rotors, especially when towing

1st Gen (1999-2006) - XK30/XK40

  • Frame rust - Severe corrosion in northern states
  • Lower ball joint failure - Critical safety issue, recall issued
  • Timing belt service - 4.7L V8 requires timing belt replacement
  • Oxygen sensor failure - Common on high-mileage vehicles

Common Issues by Generation

12th Gen (2019-Present) - E210

  • CVT hesitation - Rubber band effect when accelerating
  • Fuel pump recall - Denso fuel pump may fail
  • Rattling dashboard - Trim pieces may rattle over bumps
  • Apple CarPlay disconnects - Wireless CarPlay drops connection

11th Gen (2014-2019) - E170

  • Water pump failure - May fail prematurely on some engines
  • A/C compressor noise - Grinding or clicking from A/C
  • CVT shudder - Vibration at low speeds
  • Dashboard reflection - Glare on windshield in sunlight

10th Gen (2009-2013) - E140

  • Excessive oil consumption - 1.8L 2ZR-FE may burn oil
  • Water pump leak - Common failure point
  • Power steering noise - Whining at low speeds
  • Door handle breakage - Interior handles crack

9th Gen (2003-2008) - E120/E130

  • VVT-i rattle - Cold start rattle from VVT system
  • Rear suspension noise - Clunking from rear
  • Brake caliper sticking - Causes uneven wear
  • A/C drain clog - Water in passenger footwell

Common Issues by Generation

8th Gen (2020-Present) - B18

  • CVT shudder - Transmission vibration at low speeds
  • Infotainment bugs - NissanConnect glitches
  • Safety Shield 360 - False activation of safety features
  • Wind noise - Door seal issues

7th Gen (2013-2019) - B17

  • CVT failure - Premature transmission failure
  • Excessive oil consumption - 1.8L burns oil
  • Brake light switch - Cruise control/brake issues
  • A/C compressor - Premature failure
  • Steering lock recall - ESCL module failure

6th Gen (2007-2012) - B16

  • CVT issues - Transmission shudder, judder
  • Camshaft/crankshaft sensor - No-start condition
  • Mass air flow sensor - Engine performance issues
  • Timing chain rattle - Chain stretch on 2.5L
  • Catalytic converter - P0420 code, theft target

5th Gen (2000-2006) - B15

  • Mass air flow sensor - Common failure point
  • Pre-cat failure - 2.5L SE-R pre-cat disintegrates
  • Exhaust manifold crack - Tick noise
  • Ignition coils - Misfires, rough running
  • Thermostat housing - Coolant leak

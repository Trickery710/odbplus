Common Issues by Generation

5th Gen (2018-2020) GS/TourX

  • 3.6L timing chain - LGX chain wear and rattle
  • HiPer Strut noise - Front suspension click on GS
  • AWD haldex coupling - Service interval neglected
  • Infotainment freezing - MyBuick app connectivity drops

4th Gen Facelift (2014-2017)

  • LTG oil consumption - Piston ring seal wear
  • Turbo boost hose - Intercooler pipe cracking
  • 6-speed shudder - Torque converter shudder at light throttle
  • Door seal leak - Water ingress at base of door

4th Gen Early (2011-2013)

  • Turbo coolant pipe - Plastic coolant pipe cracking
  • Oxygen sensor failure - Upstream O2 sensor early failure
  • Water pump leak - Sealing surface corrosion
  • Power steering noise - Electric steering hum at low speed

3rd Gen (1997-2004)

  • Supercharger coupler - GS coupler wear and failure
  • Intake manifold gasket - 3800 V6 coolant leak
  • Power steering pump - Whine and fluid loss
  • BCM issues - Body control module communication faults

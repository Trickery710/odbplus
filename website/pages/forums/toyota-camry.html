Common Issues by Generation

8th Gen (2018-2024) - XV70

  • Transmission hesitation - Some owners report delayed shifts from stop
  • Fuel pump recall - Denso fuel pump may fail (recall issued)
  • Infotainment freezing - Entune/Audio Plus system lockups
  • Brake pulsation - Warped rotors reported on early models

7th Gen (2012-2017) - XV50

  • Dashboard melting - Sticky dash in hot climates (extended warranty)
  • A/C odor - Musty smell from evaporator
  • Transmission shudder - Torque converter issues on some V6 models
  • Excessive oil consumption - 2.5L engines may burn oil (2012-2014)

6th Gen (2007-2011) - XV40

  • VVT-i oil line leak - Oil leak at VVT solenoid
  • Dashboard cracking - Known issue in hot climates
  • Power steering pump whine - Common on high-mileage vehicles
  • Door handle breakage - Interior door handles crack

5th Gen (2002-2006) - XV30

  • Sludge buildup - 2.4L 2AZ-FE engine oil sludge (early models)
  • Transmission failure - Some V6 automatics fail prematurely
  • Brake light switch - Faulty switch causes multiple issues
  • Strut mount noise - Clunking over bumps

Common Issues by Generation

5th Gen (2018-Present) - RL6

  • Infotainment bugs - Touchscreen freezes, volume issues
  • 10-speed transmission - Harsh shifts, hunting for gears
  • Sliding door issues - Door won't open/close properly
  • Oil dilution - Fuel mixing with oil (recalled)
  • Battery drain - Parasitic draw from modules

4th Gen (2011-2017) - RL5

  • VCM oil consumption - 3.5L V6 burns oil with VCM active
  • Transmission shudder - Vibration during acceleration
  • Sliding door cable - Door cable snaps, door won't latch
  • Power steering pump - Whining noise, leaks
  • Spark plug blowout - Stripped threads (V6)

3rd Gen (2005-2010) - RL3/RL4

  • Transmission failure - Torque converter issues, slipping
  • VCM oil consumption - Cylinder deactivation causes oil burn
  • Power steering pump - Whining noise at low speeds
  • Sliding door failure - Motor and cable issues
  • Timing belt service - Critical maintenance item

2nd Gen (1999-2004) - RL1

  • Transmission failure - Auto trans slipping, failure common
  • Ignition switch recall - Electrical issues
  • Timing belt service - Critical maintenance item
  • Power sliding door - Motor and cable failures
  • A/C compressor failure - Age-related wear

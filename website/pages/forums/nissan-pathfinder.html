Common Issues by Generation

5th Gen (2022-Present) - R53

  • 9-speed transmission - Shift quality complaints
  • Infotainment bugs - NissanConnect glitches
  • ProPILOT issues - Driver assist false activation
  • Road noise - Tire noise complaints

4th Gen (2013-2021) - R52

  • CVT failure - Transmission shudder, whine, failure
  • Transmission cooler leak - Coolant mixes with CVT fluid
  • Timing chain rattle - 3.5L VQ35DE chain stretch
  • A/C compressor - Premature failure
  • Fuel pump recall - Fuel pump failure

3rd Gen (2005-2012) - R51

  • Timing chain rattle - 4.0L VQ40DE chain guides fail
  • Radiator failure - Internal leak mixes coolant with trans fluid
  • EVAP system - P0455/P0456 leak codes
  • Rear differential - Whine, clunk noise
  • Catalytic converter - P0420 code, theft target

2nd Gen (1996-2004) - R50

  • Distributor failure - 3.3L V6 distributor issues
  • Intake manifold gasket - Coolant leak
  • Knock sensor - Engine knock, reduced power
  • 4WD actuator - Transfer case engagement issues
  • Fuel sending unit - Inaccurate fuel gauge

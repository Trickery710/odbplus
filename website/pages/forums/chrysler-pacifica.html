2021-Present

  • Stow 'n Go seat motor - Power folding seat actuator failure

2017-2020

  • Uconnect screen - Screen going blank or requiring reboot
  • 3.6L spark plug fouling - Direct injected V6 plug carbon fouling
  • Sway bar end links - Premature failure causing clunking over bumps
  • TPMS sensor - Valve stem sensor corrosion in winter climates

Common Issues by Generation

3rd Gen (2022-Present) - D41

  • 9-speed transmission - Shift quality, hunting for gears
  • Infotainment bugs - NissanConnect glitches
  • Bed liner issues - Factory spray-in liner quality
  • Paint quality - Thin paint prone to chips

2nd Gen (2005-2021) - D40

  • Timing chain rattle - 4.0L VQ40DE chain guides fail
  • Radiator failure - Internal leak mixes coolant with trans fluid
  • Frame rust - Severe corrosion in northern states
  • Rear leaf spring noise - Leaf spring squeak
  • EVAP system - P0455/P0456 leak codes

1st Gen (1998-2004) - D22

  • Distributor failure - 3.3L V6 distributor issues
  • Timing belt service - 3.3L requires timing belt replacement
  • Supercharger issues - SC models may have boost issues
  • Fuel sending unit - Inaccurate fuel gauge
  • 4WD actuator - Transfer case engagement issues

Common Issues by Generation

2020-Present (2nd Gen)

  • Infotainment glitches - Buick Infotainment System freezes
  • Turbo hesitation - Lag on 1.4T at low RPM
  • Blind spot sensor - False alerts in certain conditions
  • Fuel economy drop - Cold weather impact on turbo

2017-2019 (1st Gen Late)

  • Oil consumption - 1.4T LUV engine burning oil
  • Transmission shudder - 6-speed auto torque converter
  • Turbocharger failure - Oil feed line blockage
  • EVAP system codes - P0442, P0446 purge valve issues

2014-2016 (1st Gen Mid)

  • Timing chain stretch - 1.4T chain and tensioner wear
  • Water pump leak - Early failure at 60-80k miles
  • MyLink system bugs - Infotainment software issues
  • Brake squeal - Front brake pad wear indicator noise

2012-2013 (1st Gen Early)

  • Turbo boost leak - Intercooler hose cracking
  • AWD coupling failure - Power Transfer Unit on AWD models
  • Hesitation on acceleration - Mass airflow sensor issues
  • Backup camera failure - Early camera unit reliability

Common Issues by Generation

2023-Present (2nd Gen Refresh)

  • CVT belt noise - Belt-driven CVT whine under load
  • Infotainment lag - Google-based system slow to respond
  • Fuel efficiency variance - Real-world vs EPA estimates gap
  • Seat material wear - Leatherette cracking on bolsters

2021-2022 (1st Gen Mid)

  • 1.2T 3-cylinder vibration - NVH at idle, engine mounts
  • CVT hesitation - Slow response from stop
  • Infotainment freezing - MyBuick app connectivity
  • AWD system fault - Rear drive module errors on cold starts

2019-2020 (1st Gen Early)

  • Turbo hesitation - Notable lag on 1.2T model
  • Transmission shudder - CVT belt wear early on
  • Parking sensor false alerts - Sensor calibration issues
  • USB port failure - Front USB ports stopping working

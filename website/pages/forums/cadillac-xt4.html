Common Issues by Generation

2023-Present (Refresh)

  • Google infotainment bugs - Android Auto/CarPlay dropouts
  • 9-speed shudder - Torque converter vibration at 40-50 mph
  • Fuel economy concerns - Real-world vs EPA on AWD models
  • Adaptive cruise behavior - Abrupt deceleration in traffic

2020-2022 (1st Gen Mid)

  • 2.0T oil consumption - LTG piston rings on some builds
  • 9-speed hunting - Gear hunting between 6th and 7th
  • Rear differential noise - AWD coupling engagement clunk
  • CUE freezing - Infotainment black screen

2018-2019 (1st Gen Early)

  • 9-speed programming - Early calibration issues, TSB fix
  • Turbo intercooler hose - Charge pipe leak at clamp
  • Sunroof drain - Drain tube blockage causing leak
  • Remote start range - Short key fob range
  • Blind spot radar - False alerts in rain or wet

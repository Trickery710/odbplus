Popular Codes

Most Searched Error Codes

Quick access to the most common diagnostic trouble codes

P0300 Powertrain

Random/Multiple Cylinder Misfire Detected

One of the most common codes indicating engine misfires across multiple cylinders.
P0171 Fuel System

System Too Lean (Bank 1)

Indicates the fuel mixture is too lean. Often caused by vacuum leaks or faulty sensors.
P0420 Emissions

Catalyst System Efficiency Below Threshold

Catalytic converter efficiency is below acceptable levels. May require replacement.
P0442 EVAP

EVAP System Small Leak Detected

Small leak detected in the evaporative emission system. Often a loose gas cap.
P0401 EGR

EGR Flow Insufficient Detected

Exhaust Gas Recirculation flow is below expected levels. Check EGR valve and passages.
P0128 Cooling

Coolant Thermostat Below Regulating Temperature

Engine is not reaching proper operating temperature. Usually indicates a stuck-open thermostat.
